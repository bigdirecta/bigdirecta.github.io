I’m Theodore, an artist working across the disciplines of photography, videography and design.

I began adult life with a Bachelor’s of Creative Writing, and since then have followed a meandering path by way of freelancing to undertaking my Honours year of Fine Arts at Monash University in 2024.

I take photos and videos of all sorts of people – mostly in music, and make social media and website content for companies. I design artwork for music releases, and create visual designs for video accompaniments to tracks, as well as live performances.
